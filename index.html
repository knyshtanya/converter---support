FAQ

  • Offline mode: When you’re offline, currency rates may be loaded from cache and can be outdated.
  • Rates look wrong: Check your internet connection and reopen the app to refresh rates.
  • How to restore purchases: Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases (if available).
  • Cancel a subscription: Manage it in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
